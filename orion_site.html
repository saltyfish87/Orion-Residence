bukit bintang golden address

THE GLITZ & GLAMOUR OF PREMIUM METROPOLITAN LIVING

Orion Residence represents beauty, grace, and sophistication. Conveniently situated between Jalan Gading & Jalan Palmer in Bukit Bintang, it is encompassed by high-end malls and renowned hotels. A towering presence of 46 floors, this establishment offers a spectacular panoramic view of the Kuala Lumpur City Center skyline. Designed by an award-winning team, the Orion Residence offers everything that luxury living represents at the heart of Kuala Lumpur metropolitan.
